A fundamental concept of modern network communication is the "Internet Protocol" address. They provide a system to unquily identify endpoints, allowing the exchange of data.

Multiple Internet websites exist to provide IP address information already, but this site is setup differently. It runs locally on campus and provides a couple benefits

  • Devices on campus without Internet connectivity can access this tool.
  • VPN users will see the address used on campus as part of the default split-tunnel VPN configuration.
